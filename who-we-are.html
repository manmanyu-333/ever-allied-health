Ever Allied Health is a mobile physiotherapy practice serving older adults and people living with disability across Melbourne's northern and eastern suburbs. We visit clients at home, because that's where life actually happens, and that's where the best care can be delivered.

Our Philosophy

Ever Allied Health was built on a simple belief: needing support doesn't change who you are. The person who built a career and raised a family is still that person, and they deserve care that reflects it. We are a mobile practice serving Melbourne's north and east. We come to you because care should fit your life—not the other way around. Whether the goal is moving more freely or reclaiming independence, our work is hands-on, practical, and focused on what matters to the individual.

The person doesn't change.

Getting older, or needing support at home, doesn't change who someone is. The same person who built a career, raised a family and lived a full life is still that person. They deserve to be treated like it.

Care that fits around a life.

We come to our clients because that's where the best care happens. In their home, in their environment, with their furniture and their routines and their independence intact.

Goals that actually matter.

One of our clients wanted to get up and dance to Elvis. He got there. That's what this work is about — the goals that seem small on paper but mean everything to the person who achieves them.

A standard, not a service.

Dignity isn't something we offer as a feature. It's the baseline. Every visit, every plan, every interaction is built around the understanding that our clients are whole people who deserve real attention and high standards.

Meet the Team

Vanessa — Principal Physiotherapist

Vanessa is a registered physiotherapist with extensive experience in aged care and disability. She founded Ever Allied Health to build something she believes in: a practice where every client is treated as a whole person, not a diagnosis or a funding category.

She specialises in functional rehabilitation, mobility, falls prevention and in-home therapy for older adults and people living with disability. Her approach is direct, thorough and grounded in what actually works for the individual in front of her.

Vanessa holds current AHPRA registration and meets all compliance requirements for working within NDIS and aged care settings, including a current police check, NDIS Worker Screening clearance, and up-to-date vaccination status. She holds professional indemnity and public liability insurance.

Growing the team

Ever Allied Health is building toward a multidisciplinary model. As we grow, we will bring on additional physiotherapists and allied health professionals who share our approach to dignified, person-centred care. All practitioners working under the Ever Allied Health banner are fully credentialled, AHPRA registered where applicable, police checked and NDIS compliant.
