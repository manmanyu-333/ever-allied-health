Who We Are

We show up, consistently.

Reliability matters in aged care and disability. We communicate clearly, attend appointments as scheduled and keep you informed. You shouldn't have to chase your provider.

We treat the whole person, not the presenting problem.

Our clients have goals, histories and opinions. We take the time to understand what matters to them, and we build treatment plans around their actual life, not a generic protocol.

We're fully credentialled and compliant.

Vanessa holds current AHPRA registration, NDIS Worker Screening clearance, a current police check and up-to-date vaccination status. All practitioners under the Ever Allied Health banner meet the same standard.

We're building for the long term.

Ever Allied Health is designed to grow into a multidisciplinary team, which means your clients can access continuity of care as their needs change.

Who We See

We work with older adults and people living with disability who want to stay in their own homes, on their own terms. Our clients come to us through a range of funding streams, and we work closely with case managers, support coordinators and care teams to make the referral process as straightforward as possible.

Learn more about funding See Service Areas

Older Adults

Clients receiving support at home who want to maintain mobility, independence and quality of life. We support people living with arthritis, Parkinson's, osteoporosis, post-surgical recovery, chronic pain and falls risk.

People Living with Disability

NDIS participants seeking physiotherapy to support daily function, mobility goals and independence. We work with self-managed, plan-managed and NDIA-managed participants.

Post-Hospital & Rehabilitation

Clients transitioning home after hospitalisation, surgery or an acute health event. We work with discharge planners and care teams to ensure a safe and supported return home.

Private Clients

Self-funded clients who prefer mobile physiotherapy in the comfort of their own home.
