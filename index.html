Our Philosophy

The person who built a career and raised a family is still that person, and they deserve care that reflects it. We are a mobile practice serving Melbourne's north and east. We come to you because care should fit your life—not the other way around. Whether the goal is moving more freely or reclaiming independence, our work is hands-on, practical, and focused on what matters to the individual.

Who We See

We work with older adults and people living with disability who want to stay in their own homes, on their own terms. Our clients come to us through a range of funding streams, and we work closely with case managers, support coordinators and care teams to make the referral process as straightforward as possible.

Older Adults

Clients receiving support at home who want to maintain mobility, independence and quality of life. We support people living with arthritis, Parkinson's, osteoporosis, post-surgical recovery, chronic pain and falls risk.

People Living with Disability

NDIS participants seeking physiotherapy to support daily function, mobility goals and independence. We work with self-managed, plan-managed and NDIA-managed participants.

Post-Hospital & Rehabilitation

Clients transitioning home after hospitalisation, surgery or an acute health event. We work with discharge planners and care teams to ensure a safe and supported return home.

Private Clients

Self-funded clients who prefer mobile physiotherapy in the comfort of their own home.

Funding and Access

We accept clients through the following funding streams:

  • NDIS (self-managed, plan-managed and NDIA-managed)
  • My Aged Care Home Care Packages (HCP)
  • Support at Home (Commonwealth Home Support Programme)
  • Chronic Disease Management / GP Management Plans (gap may apply)
  • Private (fee for service)

Not sure which funding stream applies? Contact us and we'll work through it with you.

How to Refer a Client

01

Get in touch

Call, email or complete the referral form. You don't need a formal referral document to start the conversation.

02

Tell us about your client

We'll ask about funding type, goals, mobility context, and any access considerations for a home visit.

03

We confirm availability

We'll confirm whether we can take the referral and provide an estimated start date, usually within a few business days.

04

Initial assessment

Our physiotherapist visits the client at home for a thorough initial assessment in their real environment.

05

Goal-based treatment plan

We develop a personalised plan in collaboration with the client, their family and you as the referrer. You'll be kept in the loop.

We aim to respond to all referral enquiries within one business day.
